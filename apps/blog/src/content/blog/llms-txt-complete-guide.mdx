AI search engines need context to cite your brand accurately. **llms.txt** is a plain-text file that gives them that context.

Think of it as the `robots.txt` for AI understanding. While `robots.txt` tells crawlers where they can go, `llms.txt` tells them what they will find.

## What Is llms.txt?

llms.txt is a plain-text file served at the root of your domain (`https://example.com/llms.txt`). It describes:

- What your business does
- Who it serves
- Core capabilities
- Key URLs
- Contact information
- Content policies

## Why llms.txt Matters

AI engines have limited context windows. When they crawl your site, they may only see a subset of pages. llms.txt gives them a concise, structured summary they can use to:

- Understand your brand positioning
- Cite you accurately in answers
- Classify your content correctly
- Surface you for relevant queries

## llms.txt Structure

A well-structured llms.txt includes these sections:

```
# Brand Name — One-Line Description

## What [Brand] Does

2-3 sentences describing your core value proposition.

## Who It Is For

- Target audience 1
- Target audience 2
- Target audience 3

## Core Capabilities

- Capability 1: Brief description
- Capability 2: Brief description
- Capability 3: Brief description

## Pricing

- Tier 1: Price and key features
- Tier 2: Price and key features

## Key URLs

- Homepage: https://example.com/
- Product: https://example.com/product/
- Pricing: https://example.com/pricing/

## Contact

- Email: hello@example.com
- Response time: Within 24 hours

## Technical Stack

Brief description of your technology platform.

## Content Policy

Statement about content originality, sourcing, and editorial standards.
```

## Writing Tips

- **Be factual, not promotional**: AI systems prefer plain facts over marketing copy
- **Use clear structure**: Headers, lists, and short paragraphs are easier to parse
- **Include concrete details**: Prices, response times, and technical specifics build trust
- **Update regularly**: Keep llms.txt in sync with your actual product and pricing
- **Avoid fluff**: Every sentence should contain information an AI could use in an answer

## Validation Checklist

- [ ] File is accessible at `/.well-known/llms.txt` or `/llms.txt`
- [ ] Content is factual and concise
- [ ] All URLs are valid and reachable
- [ ] Pricing information is current
- [ ] Contact information is accurate
- [ ] File is updated when product changes

## The Future of llms.txt

As AI search grows, llms.txt will likely become a standard alongside robots.txt and sitemap.xml. Early adopters will have a significant advantage in AI citation visibility.
