## What is EmDash?

EmDash represents a fundamental shift in how developers approach content management. Built on modern principles and designed for the way developers actually work, EmDash eliminates the friction that has plagued traditional CMS platforms for years.

> "EmDash isn't just another CMS — it's a complete rethinking of what content management can be when you put developers first." — The EmDash Team

### Key Features

Our platform offers a comprehensive set of features designed to make your content workflow seamless:

- **MDX Native Support**: Write your content in MDX with full access to React components within your markdown
- **Content Collections**: Type-safe content schemas ensure your data is always structured correctly
- **Static Generation**: Every page is pre-rendered at build time for optimal performance
- **Developer-First Design**: Full TypeScript support, hot module replacement, and a development experience that feels modern

### How It Works

EmDash combines the best practices from static site generators with the flexibility of modern JavaScript frameworks. Here's a quick overview:

```typescript
// Define your content schema

  type: 'content',
  schema: z.object({

    publishDate: z.date(),
```
